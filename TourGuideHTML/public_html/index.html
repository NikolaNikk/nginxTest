Sofia

Welcome to Sofia, Bulgaria's dynamic capital! With a backdrop of stunning mountains, Sofia effortlessly blends ancient history with a modern vibe. Explore diverse architecture, from Ottoman mosques to Orthodox churches. Wander cobblestone streets in the Old Town, discover Roman ruins, and dive into lively markets. Embrace the city's rich cultural tapestry, savor local cuisine, and feel the vibrant pulse of this captivating metropolis.

Paris

Welcome to Paris, the timeless jewel of France! This iconic city is a masterpiece of art, culture, and history. Marvel at the elegance of the Eiffel Tower and the grandeur of Notre-Dame Cathedral. Wander along the romantic Seine River, explore charming neighborhoods like Montmartre, and stroll through the enchanting gardens of the Tuileries. Indulge in world-class cuisine, from quaint bistros to Michelin-starred restaurants, and immerse yourself in the vibrant art scene at the Louvre or Musée d'Orsay. Paris is a city that weaves together beauty, history, and sophistication, inviting you to fall in love with its enchanting allure.

Belgrade

Welcome to Belgrade, the lively capital of Serbia! Set on the banks of the Danube and Sava rivers, this city exudes a vibrant energy and a rich historical tapestry. Explore the striking contrast between ancient fortresses like Kalemegdan, offering panoramic views, and the bustling urban life of its streets. Delve into Belgrade's diverse cultural scene, from the historic Skadarlija district's bohemian charm to the contemporary art galleries and buzzing nightlife along Strahinjića Bana street. Taste traditional Serbian cuisine in local taverns, experience the warmth of its people, and embrace the fusion of past and present in this dynamic city.
